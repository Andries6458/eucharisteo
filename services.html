Service 01

Coal Blend Optimisation

Developing optimised coal blends that maximise coke quality and resource recovery — tailored to each mine's unique coal characteristics.

Every coal deposit has a different story. Our blend optimisation process begins with a rigorous assessment of your coal's petrographic, rheological, and thermal properties — then models the optimal blend ratio to maximise coke strength, minimise rejects, and meet target specifications.

  • → Petrographic analysis & vitrinite reflectance
  • → Rheological modelling & fluidity prediction
  • → Multi-seam compatibility assessment
  • → Commercial-scale recipe recommendations
Optimised Blends Tailored per mine · Data-led
ISO / ASTM Standards Accredited method · Full reporting
Service 02

Coke Quality Analysis

Assessing key coke quality indicators against the world's most rigorous standards — because your furnace only accepts the best.

We deliver end-to-end coke quality assessment, including Micum indices (M10/M40), JIS drum indices, ASTM drop-shatter, rheology (Giesler plastometry, dilatation, CSN, Roga, XRF), and proximate analysis — all benchmarked against ISO/ASTM standards and reported in full technical detail.

Service 03

Pilot Coke Oven Commissioning

Full project management and commissioning of pilot coke ovens on client sites — enabling continuous testing and long-term quality monitoring.

Our commissioning service spans scope definition, procurement support, installation oversight, operator training, and handover. We've recently commissioned a pilot coke oven in Mozambique for one of the largest mining companies in the region — the foundation of a long-term partnership we aim to replicate across every major metallurgical coal region internationally.

Pilot Oven Deployment On-site · End-to-end delivery

Service 04

Pilot-Scale Carbonisation Testing

Controlled pilot oven tests to evaluate coking behaviour, coke strength, and product quality at pre-commercial scale — de-risking capital decisions.

Our active pilot programme in South Africa is currently testing Mozambican metallurgical coal to optimise carbonisation parameters and coke product performance ahead of commercial-scale operations. Every test cycle generates the data needed to validate — or redirect — commercial strategy before capital is committed.
