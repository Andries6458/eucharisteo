Who We Are

Redefining the metallurgical coal industry through science and stewardship.

Founded in 2017 by Leon and Annelize Dauth, Eucharisteo Trading (Pty) Ltd is a South African enterprise dedicated to redefining the thermal and metallurgical coal industry.

While commercial operations commenced in 2024, our leadership team brings over 30 years of collective expertise in coal, coal carbonisation, quality optimisation, and resource management — helping mining companies maximise resource value and extend the productive life of their operations.

Our Services

Eight disciplines. One outcome — unlocked value.

From blend design to legal advisory, our multidisciplinary offering covers every technical and commercial layer of metallurgical coal optimisation.

Coal Blend Optimisation

Developing optimised coal blends that maximise coke quality and resource recovery — tailored to each mine's unique coal characteristics.

Coke Quality Analysis

Assessing key coke quality indicators — Micum (M10/M40), JIS drum, ASTM drop-shatter, rheology, and proximate analysis against ISO/ASTM standards.

Pilot Coke Oven Commissioning

Project management and commissioning of pilot coke ovens on client sites — enabling long-term testing and quality monitoring programs.

Mine-Related Consumables

Procurement and provision of quality mining consumables, ensuring reliable supply to keep operations running without costly downtime.

Pilot-Scale Carbonisation

Controlled pilot oven tests to evaluate coking behaviour, coke strength (CRI/CSR), and product quality at pre-commercial scale.

Mine Resource Optimisation

Identifying and unlocking economic value in coal resources often overlooked — extending mine lifespans and improving profitability.
