Our Story

A legacy of metallurgical expertise, a future of sustainable innovation.

Founded in 2017 by Leon and Annelize Dauth, Eucharisteo Trading (Pty) Ltd was born from a conviction that coal — when handled with scientific rigour and sustainable intent — remains one of the most powerful levers for industrial growth across Africa.

The name Eucharisteo is drawn from the Greek for gratitude — a reflection of our founding philosophy: "Always precedes what is to come." Every engagement begins with gratitude for the resource entrusted to us, and a determination to leave the operation better than we found it.

While commercial operations commenced in 2024, the leadership team brings over three decades of collective expertise in coal carbonisation, quality optimisation, and resource management. We work alongside mining companies across South Africa and East Africa — translating complex metallurgical science into measurable commercial outcomes.
